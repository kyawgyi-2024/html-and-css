    • Welcome To Bagan Myanmar

    Bagan, Myanmar, is one of the most historically significant and visually breathtaking archaeological sites in Southeast Asia. Located in the central region of Myanmar, Bagan is home to over 2,000 ancient temples and pagodas, spanning across a vast plain that stretches along the Ayeyarwady (Irrawaddy) River.
